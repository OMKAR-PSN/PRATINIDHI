Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) - Complete Guide

Cash assistance for institutional delivery:
- Rural BPL pregnant women: Rs 1,400
- Urban BPL pregnant women: Rs 1,000
- ASHA worker incentive: Rs 600 rural, Rs 400 urban

How to register:
1. Visit nearest ASHA worker or Anganwadi centre
2. Register during first trimester (first 3 months) of pregnancy
3. Required: Aadhaar card, BPL certificate, bank account
4. Receive Mother Child Protection Card
5. At least 3 antenatal checkups required

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao:
- Scheme to prevent gender-biased sex selective elimination
- Promote education of girl child
- Districts with low Child Sex Ratio covered
- Awareness campaigns at district level

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana:
- Account for girl child below 10 years
- Interest rate: 8.2% per annum (highest among small savings)
- Minimum deposit: Rs 250 per year
- Maximum deposit: Rs 1.5 lakh per year
- Account matures when girl turns 21
- Partial withdrawal (50%) allowed at age 18 for education
- Open at post office or bank

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana:
- Rs 5,000 maternity benefit in three installments
- First installment Rs 1,000: on early registration of pregnancy
- Second installment Rs 2,000: after 6 months of pregnancy with checkup
- Third installment Rs 2,000: after child birth and first cycle vaccination
- Register at Anganwadi centre

Vaccination Schedule for Children:
- At birth: BCG, Hepatitis B, OPV
- 6 weeks: DPT, Hepatitis B, OPV, Hib, Rotavirus, PCV
- 14 weeks: DPT, OPV, Hib, Rotavirus, PCV
- 9 months: Measles/MR vaccine
- 16-24 months: DPT booster, OPV, MR second dose, JE vaccine
- All vaccines FREE at government health centres

Kanyashree Prakalpa (West Bengal):
- Annual scholarship Rs 1,000 for girls aged 13-18 in school
- One-time grant Rs 25,000 at age 18 if unmarried and continuing education
- Apply at school or block office
- Required: school enrollment proof, Aadhaar, bank account
