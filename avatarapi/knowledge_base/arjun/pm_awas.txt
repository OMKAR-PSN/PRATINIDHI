PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJANA (PMAY) - THE COMPLETE KNOWLEDGE BASE (2026 EDITION)

1. MISSION OVERVIEW AND CORE PHILOSOPHY
The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is India’s flagship housing mission.
Its primary objective is "Housing for All," ensuring every citizen has a pucca house.
A pucca house is defined as an all-weather dwelling unit with basic amenities.
Basic amenities include a hygienic kitchen, a toilet, and access to electricity and water.
The mission operates through two distinct sub-schemes: PMAY-Gramin and PMAY-Urban.
The mission has transitioned into Phase 2.0 (2024-2029) to cover additional demand.
Total investment for PMAY-Urban 2.0 alone is estimated at Rs 10 lakh crore.
The central government provides financial assistance to states and UTs for implementation.
The scheme promotes women's empowerment by requiring female ownership or co-ownership.

2. PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJANA - GRAMIN (PMAY-G) PHASE 2.0
PMAY-G Phase 2.0 covers the period from April 2024 to March 2029.
The target for this phase is to construct an additional 2 crore rural houses.
This is in addition to the original 2.95 crore houses targeted in Phase 1.0.
By early 2026, the cumulative target stands at approximately 4.95 crore houses.

2.1 FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND CONVERGENCE (GRAMIN)
Unit assistance for construction in plain areas: Rs 1,20,000.
Unit assistance for construction in hilly, difficult, or IAP districts: Rs 1,30,000.
Hilly areas include Himalayan states, Northeastern states, and the UT of J&K.
Difficult areas include LWE-affected (Left Wing Extremism) and tribal districts.
Additional support for toilet construction: Rs 12,000 via Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM-G).
Labor support via MGNREGA: 90-95 days of unskilled labor wages (approx. Rs 18,000 - 27,000).
Convergence with PM-Ujjwala Yojana: Provides free LPG connections to beneficiaries.
Convergence with Saubhagya: Provides free electricity connections to the house.
Convergence with Jal Jeevan Mission: Ensures functional household tap connections (FHTC).
Beneficiaries can opt for an institutional loan up to Rs 70,000 at a 3% lower interest rate.

2.2 ELIGIBILITY AND SELECTION (AWAAS+)
Selection is based on the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 and Awaas+ 2024 surveys.
Primary priority: Households without shelter and those living in 0, 1, or 2 room kutcha houses.
New 2026 Income Limit: Monthly household income up to Rs 15,000 (increased from Rs 10,000).
Relaxation: Owning a motorized two-wheeler (bike/scooter) no longer disqualifies a family.
Relaxation: Owning a refrigerator is no longer an automatic exclusion criterion.
Automatic Exclusion Parameters (The "Exclusion 10"):
- Families owning a motorized 3-wheeler or 4-wheeler (cars, tractors).
- Families owning a mechanized fishing boat.
- Families with any member as a regular Government employee.
- Households with non-agricultural enterprises registered with the government.
- Any family member paying Income Tax or Professional Tax.
- Families owning 2.5 acres or more of irrigated land with irrigation equipment.
- Families owning 5 acres or more of land for two or more crop seasons.

2.3 THE THREE-STAGE VALIDATION PROCESS
Stage 1: Automatic identification from SECC 2011 and Awaas+ digital databases.
Stage 2: Validation by the Gram Sabha to verify the authenticity of the claim.
Stage 3: Geo-tagging of the existing kutcha house to prevent fraudulent applications.
The final Permanent Wait List (PWL) is made public at the Panchayat level.

3. PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJANA - URBAN 2.0 (PMAY-U 2.0)
PMAY-U 2.0 was officially launched in September 2024 for a 5-year duration.
It targets the construction, purchase, or rental of 1 crore houses for urban families.
The central assistance provided for this phase is approximately Rs 2.30 lakh crore.

3.1 INCOME CATEGORIZATION FOR URBAN 2.0
Economically Weaker Section (EWS): Annual household income up to Rs 3 Lakh.
Low Income Group (LIG): Annual household income between Rs 3 Lakh and Rs 6 Lakh.
Middle Income Group (MIG): Annual household income between Rs 6 Lakh and Rs 9 Lakh.
Income is calculated as the total annual income of all earning members in the family.

3.2 THE FOUR VERTICALS OF PMAY-URBAN 2.0
Vertical 1: Beneficiary Led Construction (BLC)
- Focuses on EWS families who own their own land but have a kutcha/dilapidated house.
- Central assistance: Rs 2.5 lakh per unit.
- Carpet area: Minimum 30 sqm; States can extend this up to 45 sqm.
- If the beneficiary is landless, States/UTs may provide "pattas" (land rights).

Vertical 2: Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)
- Projects where 35% of houses are reserved for the EWS category.
- Developed by Public or Private agencies in partnership with State Governments.
- Central assistance: Rs 2.5 lakh per EWS house.
- Provides "Redeemable Housing Vouchers" to beneficiaries buying from whitelisted projects.

Vertical 3: Interest Subsidy Scheme (ISS)
- A credit-linked subsidy for EWS, LIG, and MIG families.
- Eligibility: Home loan up to Rs 25 Lakh; Property value up to Rs 35 Lakh.
- Benefit: 4% interest subsidy on the first Rs 8 Lakh of the loan.
- Maximum total benefit: Rs 1.80 Lakh.
- Subsidy is released in 5-yearly installments via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
- The loan tenure must be at least 5 years to qualify.
- Maximum carpet area allowed: 120 square meters.

Vertical 4: Affordable Rental Housing (ARH)
- Model 1: Converting existing government-funded vacant houses into rental units.
- Model 2: Private/Public agencies construct and operate rental complexes on their own land.
- Focus: Urban migrants, street vendors, industrial workers, and working women.
- Rental houses are provided with basic social infrastructure.

4. TECHNOLOGY AND MONITORING SYSTEMS
The mission emphasizes modern, green, and disaster-resilient construction.
Technology Innovation Grant (TIG): Additional grant for using innovative technologies.
TIG for ARH: Rs 3,000 per sqm from Central and Rs 2,000 per sqm from State.
Technology Sub-Mission (TISM): Facilitates the adoption of "Light House Projects."
Monitoring Portal (AwaasSoft): A comprehensive MIS for tracking every house.
Mobile App (AwaasApp/PMAY-U App): Used for inspection and geo-tagging.
Geo-tagging occurs at 5 stages: Foundation, Plinth, Lintel, Roof, and Completion.
Social Audit: Mandatory annual audit by the community to ensure transparency.
Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT): Funds are sent directly to the beneficiary's Aadhaar-linked bank account.
Aadhaar Payment Bridge System (APBS) ensures no middleman interference.

5. SPECIAL PROVISIONS AND SOCIAL INCLUSIVITY
Priority is given to Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST).
60% of rural targets are reserved for SC/ST categories.
Priority for Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) under PM-JANMAN.
Preference for Widows, Single Women, and Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan).
Transgender persons and Senior Citizens are included in the priority list.
Special focus on Safai Karmis, Street Vendors (PM-SVANidhi), and Artisans (PM-Vishwakarma).
Internal Grievance Redressal: Beneficiaries can register complaints via the PMAY portal or 1800-11-6446.

6. STEPS TO APPLY FOR PMAY-URBAN 2.0 (CITIZEN GUIDE)
Step 1: Verify eligibility based on annual household income and pucca house ownership.
Step 2: Visit the official portal pmaymis.gov.in.
Step 3: Click on 'Citizen Assessment' and enter Aadhaar/Virtual ID.
Step 4: Fill the application form with personal, bank, and income details.
Step 5: Upload mandatory documents: Identity proof, Income proof, and Property papers.
Step 6: Submit the form and note the Application ID for future tracking.
Step 7: Physical verification will be conducted by the Urban Local Body (ULB).

7. KEY COMPARISONS: PMAY 1.0 VS. PMAY 2.0
- Goal: PMAY 1.0 aimed for 3 crore houses; PMAY 2.0 aims for 3 crore more (2cr rural + 1cr urban).
- Income: Urban income limit for MIG was previously higher; 2.0 streamlines it to Rs 9 Lakh.
- Technology: 2.0 has a much larger focus on "Green Construction" and TIG grants.
- Subsidy: ISS in 2.0 offers up to Rs 1.8L, structured as a 5-year installment benefit.
- Duration: PMAY 2.0 is the definitive mission for the "Viksit Bharat 2047" vision.
