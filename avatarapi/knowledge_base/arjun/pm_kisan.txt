PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana - Complete Information

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India.
Under this scheme, income support of Rs 6000 per year is provided to all 
farmer families across the country in three equal installments of Rs 2000 each.

Eligibility:
- All landholding farmer families with cultivable land
- Family means husband, wife and minor children
- Both institutional landholders and small and marginal farmers are eligible

How to Register:
- Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
- Visit pmkisan.gov.in website
- Contact local Patwari/Revenue Officer
- Required documents: Aadhaar card, bank account, land records

Payment Process:
- Money transferred directly to bank account via DBT
- Three installments: April-July, August-November, December-March
- Amount: Rs 2000 per installment, Rs 6000 per year total

eKYC is mandatory for receiving benefits.
Farmers can check status at pmkisan.gov.in
Helpline: 155261 or 1800115526

Jan Dhan Yojana - How to open account:
- Visit any bank branch or Business Correspondent
- Fill account opening form
- Submit Aadhaar card or other identity proof
- No minimum balance required
- Get free RuPay debit card
- Overdraft facility up to Rs 10,000 after 6 months

PM Awas Yojana Gramin:
- Free pucca house for homeless and kutcha house residents
- Rs 1,20,000 assistance in plains, Rs 1,30,000 in hilly areas
- Apply through Gram Panchayat or online at pmayg.nic.in
- Eligibility: BPL families, SC/ST, minorities, women

Ration Card:
- Apply at local PDS office or online at state portal
- Required: Aadhaar, residence proof, family details
- Entitlement: 5kg food grains per person per month at subsidized rate
- Green card: BPL families, Yellow card: AAY (poorest) families
